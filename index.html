About Inflight Fitness

Originally a custom aircraft parts manufacturer, Inflight Fitness has been serving the fitness industry since the mid 1990s as a designer, manufacturer and distributor of commercial-grade strength training equipment. Our products combines the quality demanded by the aircraft industry with the experience of manufacturing strength training equipment to bring commercial-grade, budget-friendly to our customers.

Over the last twenty five years, we have expanded our original Pacific Fitness SE Strength Line and now offer Dual Selectorized, Functional Trainers, Multi-Gyms and Free Weight machines to the multi-family, corporate municipality, defense and education sectors across the globe.
